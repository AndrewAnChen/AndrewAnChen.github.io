About

I’m currently a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in biostatistics at the University of Pennsylvania studying under Drs. Taki Shinohara and Haochang Shou. I’m a proud member of the Penn Statistics in Imaging and Visualization Endeavor (PennSIVE), a Center of Excellence specializing in statistical methods for biomedical image analysis. I’m also involved in a number of collaborations with members of Center for Biomedical Image Computing and Analytics (CBICA) and the Child Mind Institute. My primary research interests include statistical harmonization, covariance regression, distributed data analysis, and distance statistics.
